A few words of Swedish

Tap any phrase to hear it. Swedes speak English beautifully — but a warm tack goes a long way.

å sounds like the o in "more" → oh
ä sounds like the e in "bed" → eh
ö like the u in "fur" (rounded) → ur
sk before e/i/y/ä/ö → a soft "sh" (sked = "shehd")
j is a y sound → ja = "yah"
g before e/i/y → often a y → "gärna" = "YAIR-na"
